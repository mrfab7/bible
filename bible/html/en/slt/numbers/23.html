And Balak will say to Balaam, What didst thou to me? To curse mine enemies I took thee, and behold, praising, thou didst praise.

And he will answer and say, What Jehovah shall put in my mouth shall I not watch, to speak?

God is not man, and will he lie? and the son of man, and will he grieve? Did he say, and will he not do? and speak, and will he not establish?

Behold, I was taken to praise: and he praised and I shall not turn it back.

He saw not iniquity in Jacob and he saw not sorrow in Israel: Jehovah his God with him, and the shouts of a king in them.

God bringing them out of Egypt; as the swiftness of the buffalo to him.

For no enchantment in Jacob, and no divination in Israel; according to the time it will be said to Jacob and to Israel, What did God do!
