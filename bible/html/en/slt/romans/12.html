In study not slothful; boiling in the spirit; serving the Lord;

Rejoicing in hope; holding out under pressure; persevering in prayer;

Participating in the necessities of the holy ones; pursuing hospitality.

Praise them driving you out: praise ye, and curse not.

To rejoice with the rejoicing, and weep with the weeping.

Thinking the same towards one another. Not thinking high things but being led by humble things. Be not wise with yourselves.

If possible of you, living peaceably with all men.

Avenging not yourselves, beloved, but give ye place to anger: for it has been written, Vengeance to me; I will repay, says the Lord.

If therefore thine enemy hunger, feed him; if he thirst, give him drink: for doing this, thou shalt heap up coals of fire on his head.

Be not overcome of evil, but overcome evil with good.
