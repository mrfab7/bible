And thou saidst in that day, I will praise thee, O Jehovah: for thou wert angry with me, thine anger will turn back and thou wilt comfort me,

Behold God my salvation; I will trust and not be afraid: for my strength and song is Jah Jehovah, and he will be to me for salvation.

Ye drew water with gladness from the fountains of salvation.

And ye said in that day, Praise ye to Jehovah, call upon his name; make known his deeds among the peoples; keep ye in remembrance that his name was exalted.

Strike the chords to Jehovah, for he did a lifting up: this being known in all the earth.

Cry out and shout for joy thou inhabitress of Zion, for great in the midst of thee the Holy One of Israel.
