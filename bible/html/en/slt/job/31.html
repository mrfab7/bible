I cut out a covenant for mine eyes; and why shall I show myself attentive to a virgin?

And what the portion of God from above? and the inheritance of the Almighty from the heights?

Is not destruction to the evil one? and strangeness to those working iniquity?

Will he not see my way, and number all my steps?

If I went with vanity, and my foot will haste upon deceit;

He shall weigh me in balances of justice, and God will know mine integrity.

If my going will incline from the way, and my heart went after mine eyes, and a blemish did cleave upon my hands:

I will sow and another shall eat; and my offspring shall be rooted up.

If my heart was seduced by woman, and I laid wait at the door of my neighbor;

My wife shall grind for another, and others shall bow down upon her.

For this is crime, and this is iniquity for the judges.

For it is a fire; it will devour even to destruction, and will root out upon all mine increase.

If I shall despise the judgment of my servant and my maid in their contending with me;

And what shall I do when God shall rise up? And when he shall review, what shall I turn back to him?

Did not he making me in the womb, make him? And he will prepare us in one belly.
