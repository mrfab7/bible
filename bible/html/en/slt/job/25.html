And Bildad the Shuhite will answer and say,

Dominion and fear with him; he made peace in his heights.

Is there a number to his troops? and upon whom shall not his light arise?

And how shall man be justified with God? and how shall he born of woman be clean?

Behold even to the moon, and it shall not shine; and the stars were not clean in his eyes.

How much less man, a worm; and the son of man, a worm.
