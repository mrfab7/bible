Nohah the fourth, and Rapha the fifth.

And the sons to Bela will be Adar and Gera and Abihud,

And Abishua, and Naaman, and Ahoah,

And Gera, and Shephuphan, and Huram.

And these the sons of Ehud: these the heads of the fathers to the inhabitants of Geba; and they will carry them away captive to Manahath.

And Naaman and Ahiah and Gera, he carried them away captive; and he begat Uzza and Ahihud.

And Shaharaim begat in the field of Moab from his sending them away; Hushim and Baara his wives.

And he will beget from Hodesh his wife, Jobab, and Zibia, and Mesha, and Malcham,

And Jeuz and Shachiah and Mirma. These his sons, heads of the fathers,

And from Hushim he begat Abitub and Elpaal.

The sons of Elpaal: Eber, and Misham, and Shamei, he built Ono and Lod, and her daughters.

And the sons of Micah: Pithon, and Melech, and Tarea, and Ahaz.
