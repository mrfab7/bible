They caused the naked to go without clothing, and from the hungry they took away the sheaf;

Between their walls they will press out oil; they trod their wine-presses, and they will thirst.

From the city men will groan, and the soul of the wounded will cry out: and God will not set folly.

They were among the rebels of light; they knew not his ways, and they dwelt not in his beaten paths.

The murderer will rise by light; he will kill the poor and needy; and in the night he will be as a thief.

And the eye of the adulterer watched the twilight, saying, Eye shall not perceive me: and secretly he will set the face.

To break through houses in darkness in the day they shut up for themselves: they knew not light.

For the morning altogether to them the shadow of death: if it shall be known, the terrors of the shadow of death.

He is swift upon the face of the waters; their portion will be cursed in the earth: he will not see the way of the vineyards.

Dryness, also heat, will strip the snow waters: hades him sinning.

The womb shall forget him; the worm sucked him; he shall be no more remembered: and iniquity shall be broken as a tree.

He did evil to the barren; she will not bear: and he will not do good to the widow.

And he drew the mighty in his power: he will rise up and none shall be sure in living.
